I'm a digital product designer passionate about solving complex problems with delightful, human-centered solutions.

I designed at Royal Caribbean as part of their Excalibur program, a digital transformation initiative focusing on special projects for guest as well as crew experiences.

Prior to that, I worked as a data analyst and project manager at tech companies ranging from small startups to Fortune 500 corporations, moved from Silicon Valley to Shanghai to South Florida, and grew increasingly intrigued by product design all along the way. While my career path thus far looks a bit more like a jungle gym than a ladder, it has enabled me to bring a unique perspective to building great products today. (Besides, jungle gyms are more fun!)

Outside of work, I enjoy volunteering as an ESL teacher (I'm certified by Cambridge University, and had a brief stint teaching English abroad in China), planning my next vacation, consuming artisinal ice cream, and going down Internet rabbit holes.

Please feel free to reach out and get to know me!
